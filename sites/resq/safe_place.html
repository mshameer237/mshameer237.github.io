Features

Get room level position accuracy, compared to street level provided by GPS

If you have elderly Parents staying alone you can setup to get alerts when they leave and return to the house

Configure to get alerts when your Child enter & exit School, School Bus, Music Class and others

Uses existing Wi-Fi and Apple iBeacon Technology

Weather Proofed Location Buttons are sold separately for area not covered by Wi-Fi like Soccer Field and other outdoor activities

Automatically notify three designated users when you enter/exit an indoor area, like Home, Office & College

Need not be connected to the Wi-Fi (Android Only) and no drain on your battery like other products using GPS based solutions

Family

  • Geo-Fencing to notify your designated contacts when you ENTER or EXIT your configured Safe Place(s)
  • A simple use case like, getting automatic notifications when you’re Child Enters or Exits school, Home, School Bus and other places instead of waiting for their call or SMS.

How Do I Configure A Wi-fi Based Safe Place?

  • You should be in the vicinity of the specific Wi-Fi, for iPhone you need to be connected to that Wi-Fi
  • Click on the My Safe Place(s) icon and click on the + button and select the Wi-Fi
  • Choose a ‘Location Name’ like Home, School, Library and Others
  • Pick the Wi-Fi Name from the list
  • Add a message like ‘John at School’so others can figure out it is you
  • Fill in the address of the Location, use the Map pin to position if your address is does not show up on the search
  • Describe the Location like Apartment Number, Floor No
  • Fill in your three designated contacts, make sure all three address used are valid ResQ Button App users.

How it Works

  • Uses the existing Wi-Fi and Apple iBeacon Technology.

  • Android Phones need not be connected to the Wi-Fi.
    iPhone requires a connected session

  • Configure your Safe Place either Wi-Fi based or ResQ Button based

    • For the Wi-Fi based pick the SSID which has the largest coverage
    • For ResQ Button based add the Group Number it belongs to
    • Please configure the Location Name & Message as descriptively as you require

  • As soon as the phone detects the specific Wi-Fi or Location ResQ Button for the first time
    an Entry Alert is sent out.
    2 Minutes after the Phone leaves the specific Wi-Fi or Location ResQ Button
    area an Exit Alert is sent out

  • All the three designated contacts are notified that the Phone has entered/
    exited with the Location Name & Message as specified during the configuration
    So no more waiting for a SMS from your loved ones when they reached their Safe Place

  • To Geo-Fence a large area like a Mall or Soccer Field, you will require multiple Location Buttons

    • Configure all Location Buttons for an area under the same Group Number.
    • Make ResQ Button My Safe Place users to configure their phones with the Group Number
    • Even additional Location
    • Make users configure ResQ Button Based Safe Place with that Group Number
    • As the user moves from one Location Button range to another one because of same group number only one Entry & Exit alert will be generated
    • When the phone is out of range of all the same Group Number an Exit alert is generated

  • If there is no network connectivity to send an Entry & Exit notification

    • Phone will save the information locally and send it out when connected to network.
    • When the notifications are received it will tell the actual time the Entry & Exit occurred
