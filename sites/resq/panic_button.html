What Is Panic Resq Button?

  • Panic ResQ Button is a wearable device about the size of a water bottle cap when activated broadcasts distress signal continuously.
  • The Distress Signal is picked up by any ResQ Button App phone in the vicinity and forwarded to our Highly Redundant Cloud Computers.
  • The Cloud Computers notifies your designated contacts through SMS & Push notifications with a real-time map
  • If you or some other ResQ Button App user has provided SMS Capable Police Phone number of your current location we will automatically inform them and they will get similar map as your emergency contacts
  • If configured, Cloud Computers starts locating other ResQ Button App users near you and ask them to help you. Remember it is faster for your neighbor to reach and help you
  • If you are on a business trip, any emergencies automatically your corporate security team is notified
  • Works across the world as long as network connection is available, user Crowd Sourcing & Crowd Sharing model
  • With Selfie culture, number of people willing to help is small, so your own personal safety is at your fingertips
  • Even if you do not own a Panic ResQ Button, download and configure the ResQ Button App on your phone to help someone in distress
  • ResQ Button App Phone and Cloud Computers together used to identify indoor locations, so useful to get help to the floor or Apartment as required

What Are The Requirements?

  • Purchase a Panic ResQ Button
  • Configure it with a ResQ Button App phone with the following information
    • Button Owner Name
    • Phone Number
    • Disable and Emergency PIN’s
    • Up to 6 Emergency Contacts they may or may not be a ResQ Button App users, but own a Smart Phone with network connectivity
  • If you do not own a phone with ResQ Button App, make sure you will be in the vicinity of a ResQ Button App device, like School Children in vicinity of Teachers with ResQ Button App installed phones
  • ResQ Button App requires the Location Services, Network & Bluetooth to be on all the time, it is neither a security risk nor a Battery Hog for keeping Bluetooth on all the time, if you take care of certain preventive measures

How Do I Configure A Panic Resq Button?

  • Buy a Panic ResQ Button
  • Make sure you have network connectivity and download the App from the respective store
  • After registration click on the My ResQ Button(s) icon on the App
  • Click the + button on the App bottom right hand corner
  • Press and Hold the Logo for 3 seconds for the Green LED to come on
  • Keep the Panic ResQ Button on top of your Phone Screen
  • Phone will negotiate with the Panic ResQ Button to determine the Serial Number, Model Number and other things, this process will take about 45 seconds to complete
  • If everything is okay then the App will move to the Add ResQ Button screen
  • Configure your button with the required information like
    • Button Nick Name like Keychain, Necklace etc
    • Change the Button Owner details if the button is for someone else other than you
    • Enter 4-digit Disable and Emergency PIN, they both should be different
    • Disable PIN is to disable the Panic Alarm during false activations
    • If you are forced to enter the Disable PIN, enter the emergency PIN, we will display as alarm resolved, but internally we will notify your emergency contacts and Police
    • Fill in details about your 6 Emergency Contacts, if you don’t know their ResQ Button App user ID’s just enter their Phone numbers

Why You Need It

  • Most emergencies
    are by surprise

    with less reaction time for
    victims to call Police or
    Ambulance for help, they also
    find it hard to describe their location and type.

  • With Panic ResQ Button hardware
    and App combined

    pressing the Logo on the Panic ResQ Button starts broadcasting S.O.S signal which can be picked by any ResQ Button App Phone in the vicinity and sent to ResQ Cloud Computers. Even if your phone is disabled by the attacker signal reaches the ResQ Cloud Computers through other ResQ Button App phones in the vicinity.

  • All your emergency contacts are notified through SMS and Push Notifications

    If Notify other ResQ Button App users configured, the ResQ Cloud Computers locates potential volunteers near you who can help including your Campus friends & Security if present. Remember in an emergency it is faster for your neighbor to help you.

  • Police SMS numbers are crowd sourced

    if you or someone has provided the Police SMS number for the incident jurisdiction, a SMS with link to the incident is sent to Police and updated real-time as location changes.

  • Using Wi-Fi & other Location Identifiers near you

    ResQ Cloud is able to pin-point your location to a Floor, Apartment or Room specific in the notifications sent out to your emergency contacts and Police. GPS are street level information only.

How it Works

  • Press Logo in an emergency for 3 seconds

  • Distress signal is picked up by any ResQ Button App phone in
    30m+ non-obstructive vicinity within two seconds.

  • ResQ Button App will forward the distress signal to the
    ResQ Cloud Engine through Dataplan or SMS

  • Your emergency contacts are notified after the
    expiry of the 30 seconds.

  • Incidents occuring in known Office/College Campus the
    local Emergency Response Team's are notified.

  • If Notify Volunteer option is selected, ResQ Cloud Engine starts pinging other ResQ Button App
    Phones in 100m - 1000m radius to see if anyone could help.

  • Since each incident jurisdiction have a different SMS capable phone numbers for police,
    as information is populated, local police is informed by SMS.
