MARGIN TRADING FUNDING

Geojit Financial Services Ltd. offers Margin Trading Funding facility to all customers under the scheme of Margin Funding approved by SEBI.

Key Features of the scheme are as follows:

  1. Easy and prompt account opening formalities
  2. No perocessing fees
  3. Margin funding of up to 50% of the purchase value
  4. Shares purchased are credited to customers’ Demat account maintained specifically for the purpose of Margin Trading.
  5. Bonus, and Rights are also directly credited to the customers said Demat account. Similarly dividend shall be credited to the bank account stated in the said Demat account.
  6. Margin calls are made if there is fall of 10% in the Margin Funded Portfolio. Margins can be replenished by cheque or by transferring funds from Normal Trading account.
  7. Liquidation will occur if margins are not topped up on a 20% fall in the Margin Funded Portfolio. In such an event liquidation will be at the lenders discretion.
  8. This is a product to facilitate investments for short term. Interest is charged on monthly rests at the rate of 1.66% per month.
  9. Purchases can be made only against clear funds
  10. We provide Margin Funding only for scrips traded at the NSE only.
