Rapid application development (RAD) focuses on how to build software solutions for your clients and end-users using predefined prototyping techniques and tools to expedite software application development. RAD uses minimal planning in favor of rapid prototyping.

When to use RAD?

RAD should be used when there is a need to build an internal business tool or even a customer-facing portal, like an app or website that can be modularized in 2-3 months of time which can deliver a better experience to your end-user.
