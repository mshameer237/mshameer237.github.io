About Us

Pippin Title is driven to provide a streamlined solution to the complexities
of title search and examination.

Our team has taken a fresh and innovative approach to this cumbersome and expensive process. Our cutting edge technological platform is built on a foundation of over 15 years of industry experience and a nationwide network of on-site abstractors. Pippin Title is committed to providing efficient and cost-effective solutions for your title needs every step of the way.

Innovative Features of Our Platform

  • Nationwide Coverage
  • Transparency and Scalability
  • Rapid Turnaround

Nationwide Coverage

  • We offer comprehensive coverage on the ground in every county across all 50 states
  • Our extensive network of over 500 local abstractors is made up of licensed and insured professionals
  • Each abstractor is committed to getting you the document you need – online or through physical document retrieval

Transparency and Scalability

  • Our platform offers quick order entry and pricing, providing a clear breakdown of your request
  • With our real time tracking feature you are always kept up to date with the most recent developments on your order
  • Our team is equipped to handle virtually any size order that you require, even if you need several hundred orders per week in multiple different states

Rapid Turnaround

  • Our system is designed to return orders as quickly as possible so you can pass that efficiency on to your customers
  • Current owner searches, deed reports and update bring-down requests are usually returned within 24 hours
  • Two owner searches are usually returned within 48 hours
  • Full Owner searches for 30 or 60 years are usually returned within 72 hours

Customer Satisfaction

  • Our ultra-fast customer service is a reflection of our values and sets us apart in the title industry
  • We take pride in being responsive and helpful no matter what your needs may be
  • Our user interface is designed with you in mind in order to provide a clean and seamless customer experience
